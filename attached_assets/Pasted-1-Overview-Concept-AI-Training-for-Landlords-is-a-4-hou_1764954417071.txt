1. Overview / Concept

AI Training for Landlords is a 4-hour, live, hands-on workshop designed to teach small and midsize landlords how to confidently use AI tools to:

Fill vacancies faster

Create stronger marketing

Understand credit reports

Interpret new landlord/tenant laws

Reduce overwhelm with practical workflows

Use LeaseShield as the “easy button” for all of the above

Program Structure

Paid 4-hour live workshop — $200 per attendee

Free 1-hour monthly onboarding session for LeaseShield subscribers

This creates a clean funnel from free → paid → subscription retention.
