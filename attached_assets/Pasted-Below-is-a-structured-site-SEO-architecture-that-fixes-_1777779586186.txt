1. Core positioning (lock this first)

You need one clear category statement that everything ladders into:

“Simple rental management system for landlords — from application and screening to rent collection and compliance.”

This lets you rank for:

* property management software (long-tail)
* tenant screening
* rent collection
* landlord tools

Without competing head-on with enterprise platforms.

2. Site architecture (SEO + conversion aligned)

A. Homepage (conversion + broad keyword capture)

Primary keyword targets:

* landlord software
* rental management system
* tenant screening and rent collection

Structure:

Hero (choose one angle):

* “Run your entire rental process in one place”
    OR
* “From application to rent collection—without the complexity”

Immediately show workflow:
Application → Screening → Decoder → Lease → Rent Collection

Sections:

1. “Start with an application”
2. “Run a proper background check”
3. “Understand the report (decoder)”
4. “Generate compliant leases/notices”
5. “Collect rent and track everything”

Why this matters for SEO:

* Covers multiple keyword clusters on one page
* Signals topical authority to Google
* Reduces bounce (clear path)

3. Blog strategy (how you actually rank)

You don’t win SEO with product pages alone.

You win with problem-based content tied to your features.

⸻

High-value blog clusters:

Cluster 1: Screening confusion

* “How to read a tenant background check”
* “What shows up on a tenant screening report?”
* “Do eviction records show up on background checks?”

→ links to decoder page

⸻

Cluster 2: Risk + liability (your strongest sales angle)

* “What happens if you rent to a tenant with a criminal record?”
* “Landlord liability for tenant actions”
* “Why most background checks miss records”

→ links to screening page

⸻

Cluster 3: Rent collection

* “Best way to collect rent online for small landlords”
* “How to track rent payments without software”
* “What to do if a tenant stops paying rent”

→ links to rent collection page

⸻

Cluster 4: Compliance

* “Pre-adverse action letter for landlords”
* “FCRA rules for tenant screening”
* “Fair housing mistakes landlords make”

→ links to decoder + forms

Not random tools.
