CITY-LEVEL TENANT SCREENING OVERRIDES

(Apply IN ADDITION to state rules)

ARIZONA
Phoenix

CANNOT Deny Based On:
• Arrests without conviction
• Criminal records older than 7 years unless directly related to safety

Must Follow These Rules:
• Criminal history must have a clear, documented connection to housing safety
• Blanket criminal bans increase fair housing risk

CALIFORNIA
Los Angeles

CANNOT Deny Based On:
• Criminal history older than 7 years
• Eviction filings without a court judgment
• Participation in rental assistance programs

Must Follow These Rules:
• Mandatory individualized criminal assessment
• Written explanation required if denying based on criminal history
• Eviction lookback heavily restricted

San Francisco

CANNOT Deny Based On:
• Any arrest not resulting in conviction
• Convictions older than 7 years
• Non-violent convictions unless directly related to tenancy
• Eviction filings without judgment

Must Follow These Rules:
• Fair Chance Ordinance applies
• Must delay criminal screening until later in process
• Written pre-adverse and final adverse notices required

Oakland

CANNOT Deny Based On:
• Criminal history older than 7 years
• Sealed, dismissed, or expunged records
• Eviction filings

Must Follow These Rules:
• Must allow applicant to submit mitigating evidence
• Denials must be narrowly tailored

FLORIDA

(No major city-level overrides currently beyond state law)

IDAHO

(No city-level fair housing expansions beyond state law)

ILLINOIS
Chicago

CANNOT Deny Based On:
• Arrests without conviction
• Convictions older than 3 years (with narrow exceptions)
• Sealed or expunged records
• Eviction filings without judgment
• Housing vouchers or lawful source of income

Must Follow These Rules:
• Criminal screening only after conditional approval
• Written individualized assessment required
• Must allow applicant response before final denial

MICHIGAN
Detroit

CANNOT Deny Based On:
• Arrests without conviction
• Sealed or expunged records

Must Follow These Rules:
• Criminal history must be directly related to housing safety
• Adverse action notice required

NEVADA
Las Vegas / Clark County

CANNOT Deny Based On:
• Housing vouchers or rental assistance
• Arrests without conviction

Must Follow These Rules:
• Source of income protection enforced locally
• Uniform screening criteria required

NEW MEXICO
Albuquerque

CANNOT Deny Based On:
• Arrests without conviction
• Sealed or expunged records
• Housing vouchers
• Eviction filings without judgment

Must Follow These Rules:
• Criminal history must show direct housing risk
• Individualized assessment required

NORTH CAROLINA
Charlotte

CANNOT Deny Based On:
• Arrests without conviction

Must Follow These Rules:
• Criminal history policies must avoid disparate impact
