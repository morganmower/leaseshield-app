5️⃣ California — Major Improvement, One Remaining Structural Risk

California

Your AB 1482 gating logic is the right move.

However:

You are currently asking:

“Built before Feb 1, 2015? → Likely covered”

That’s directionally correct but legally imprecise.

Coverage depends on:

Certificate of occupancy date

Corporate ownership rules

Notice of exemption filing

Single-family home ownership structure

If you present this as binary logic, landlords may incorrectly self-classify.

Safer approach:

Instead of:

“Likely covered”

Use:

“Properties built before Feb 1, 2015 are generally covered unless exempt. Confirm exemption status.”

Force user acknowledgment before proceeding.

You cannot algorithmically determine AB 1482 compliance without property-level data you don’t collect.

Your gating should be advisory + acknowledgment, not deterministic.
