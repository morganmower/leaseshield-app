7) FAQ section (add or replace)

Put this near the bottom.

FAQ 1

Q: Do I need to use this every month?
A: No. Most landlords don’t use LeaseShield every day — they keep it active so it’s there when an applicant comes up.

FAQ 2

Q: Does LeaseShield replace Western Verify screening?
A: No. Western Verify provides the screening reports. LeaseShield helps you collect applications, send screening, and interpret results clearly.

FAQ 3

Q: Can I cancel anytime?
A: Yes. You can cancel anytime. No contracts.

FAQ 4 (optional but helpful)

Q: What if I only have a few units?
A: That’s common. LeaseShield is built for small and midsize landlords who want clarity and consistency when screening.
