Why it feels like too much (even if it isn’t)

2.1 Everything is competing for attention

Right now:

* screening
* decoder
* rent
* compliance
* forms

All get similar weight.

⸻

Result:

User has to decide:

“What matters most here?”

2.2 Lack of clear “reading lanes”

High-performing pages have:

* skim layer (headlines only)
* medium layer (short explanations)
* deep layer (details)

⸻

Your issue:

Too much content sits in the middle layer

Not enough:

* punchy headlines
* scannable chunks

⸻

You introduced the workflow (good), but:

* content doesn’t consistently reinforce it
* sections feel slightly independent

⸻

So instead of:

“Step 1 → Step 2 → Step 3”

It feels like:

“Here are several important things”

MORE in the right places:

* rent collection benefits

LESS in the wrong places:

* repeated general explanations
